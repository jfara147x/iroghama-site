About Us

Iroghama was founded by Osahon Idemudia in 2020. It has since evolved from its initial focus on construction and real estate matters into a multi-specialisation, full service firm geared to facilitate corporate and commercial businesses in Nigeria and the world, across its various practice areas.

The firm's corporate practice is supported by a dedicated litigation and alternative dispute resolution team, and also by a company secretarial department, which provides a full range of company secretarial services. Our vision is to be the global law firm of choice for the world's leading businesses of today and the future.

Today our firm enjoys a position at the forefront of our profession. We are proud of what we achieve every day, working in collaboration and partnership with our clients and wider stakeholders to secure their long-term, sustainable success. But we are not complacent.

Our strategy recognises that the world we operate in is changing and that we too must be ready to change if we are to continue to shape our sector:
